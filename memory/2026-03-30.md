- Addition/Subtraction games (1-20)
- Skip counting games
- Word flashcards
- Stars & rewards system
- Day streak tracking
- Badges to earn
- Printable math & reading worksheets
- Fire tablet optimized

**App 2: Little Dentist** (http://localhost:9092/teeth.html)
- Learn teeth anatomy (28 teeth: incisors, canines, premolars, molars)
- Interactive mouth diagram - tap to learn each tooth
- Quiz mode - identify tooth types and locations
- Cavity repair game - drill and fill cavities
- Earns stars for correct answers
