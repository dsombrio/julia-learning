**KIDS ACTIVITIES (4yo daughter)**
Lesson ideas for this week:

Math - Counting with Toys:
- What: Practice counting 1-20 using her favorite toys (stuffed animals, blocks, dolls)
- How: Make it a game - "How many dinosaurs can we line up?" Reward with a small treat for accuracy
- Materials: Her existing toys, paper for numbers if wanted

Science - Sink or Float:
- What: Learn about buoyancy with water play
- How: Fill a bowl with water, gather small objects (plastic cup, sponge, wooden block, coin). Ask her to predict before each drop
- Materials: Bowl, water, various household objects (child-safe)

Finance - "My Piggy Bank":
- What: Introduce saving vs spending
- How: Use three containers (save/spend/give). When she receives money, let her divide it. Explain "save for later" vs "buy now"
- Materials: 3 jars/containers, labels

Local events this weekend: Check The Eagle (theeagle.com) or Bryan-College Station parks department for weekend activities. Spring weather ideal for outdoor play at Veterans Park or College Station parks.
